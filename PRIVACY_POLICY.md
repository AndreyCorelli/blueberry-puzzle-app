# Privacy Policy

**Blueberry Puzzle** is a free mobile game.  
This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
The app:
- does not collect personal information
- does not use analytics
- does not show ads
- does not use tracking technologies
- does not require user accounts or login

## Third-Party Services
The app does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

## Children’s Privacy
This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

## Changes
If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL.

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer via the project’s GitHub repository.
